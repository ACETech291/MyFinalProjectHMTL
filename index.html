BMW 3 Series

The BMW 3 Series is a compact luxury sedan that has been in production since 1975. It’s available in several models including the 330i, 330e, M340i, and M3. The 2024 model utilizes a 2.0-liter BMW TwinPower Turbo inline 4-cylinder gasoline engine capable of generating up to 255 horsepower, and a 3.0-liter inline 6-cylinder BMW M TwinPower Turbo gasoline engine that boasts a maximum output of 382 horsepower. The 3 Series can seat up to five passengers and offers a range of safety features including Frontal Collision Warning, Lane Departure Warning, and Active Blind Spot Detection.

BMW iX3

The BMW iX3 is an all-electric family SUV that exudes quality. It’s essentially an X3 with electric power, built on the same platform and with broadly similar looks. The iX3 M Sport looks and feels sportier than ever, thanks to the standard-feature M Sport package and its rear-wheel drive powertrain. It has a range of up to 285 miles and electricity consumption from 3.3 mi/kWh. The iX3 can drive up to 461 kilometers on a full charge, thanks to an efficient drivetrain concept, aerodynamic design, and a powerful high-voltage battery.

BMW XM 2024

The 2024 BMW XM is a luxury, high-performance plug-in-hybrid SUV that seats five people. It utilizes a 4.4-liter V-8 BMW M TwinPower Turbo combustion engine combined with a high-performance electric motor, producing up to 738 horsepower. The XM can drive up to ~30 miles on all-electric power. It includes the BMW Curved Display, perfect for controlling the iDrive 8 Operating System and Intelligent Personal Assistant. The XM is nearly as quick as an M3 sports sedan but offers similar luxury as the 7-series, making this SUV unique.

BMW Z4 sDriver20i

The BMW Z4 sDrive20i is a rear-wheel-drive vehicle with a 2-door convertible/cabriolet bodyshell. It is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine. This engine features double overhead camshaft valve gear, a 4-cylinder layout, and 4 valves per cylinder. It can accelerate from 0 to 62 mph in 6.6 seconds and has a top speed of 149 mph.
